Pfeiffer Landscaping, LLC

Pfeiffer Landscaping, LLC is a licensed residential design-build firm creating quality landscapes in the Seattle area since 1986. Lisa Pfeiffer, owner, has over 25 years of experience in commercial and residential design, construction and maintenance, giving her a unique and complete perspective for creative solutions to landscape design.
